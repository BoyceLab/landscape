# Resources by readiness level

Support is most useful when it meets an organization where it is. The directory below maps established organizations and services to the stages they help with, drawn from the maturity model. Use the [self-assessment](self-assessment.md) to find your level, then start with the resources matched to it.

## Named partners in the support network

Several of these organizations recur across tracks because they work specifically on research readiness:

- **Rare Epilepsy Network (REN).** Entry point into the rare epilepsy research ecosystem.
- **COMBINEDBrain.** Pools data and biospecimens across rare neuro groups to reach data sharing faster.
- **NORD.** Umbrella organization for nonprofit capacity, registries, and advocacy.
- **NINDS and NIH / NCATS.** Federal research funding and rare disease data infrastructure.
- **HHS / FDA.** Patient-focused drug development guidance and meetings.
- **Data for the Common Good and RDCA-DAP.** Vetted partners for governed, harmonized data sharing.
