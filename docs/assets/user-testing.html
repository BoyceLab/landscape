Help us test the Living Atlas

This takes about fifteen minutes and you can do it on your own time. Pick the role that best describes you, work through a few short tasks in the Atlas, and tell us what worked and what did not. Open the Atlas in a second tab so you can switch back and forth.

Nothing is recorded automatically. At the end you choose how to send your feedback: copy it, download it, or open a pre-filled GitHub submission.
