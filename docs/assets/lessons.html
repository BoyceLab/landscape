What did not work, and what you would do differently

The hardest won knowledge in this community is what to avoid. Share a lesson from your own research experience so the next organization at your stage does not repeat it. This is about fit, not fault: most lessons are a mismatch between an approach and where an organization was, not wrongdoing by anyone.

Nothing publishes automatically. Naming a vendor, product, or other organization is not part of this form. A reviewer removes any names or personal health information that slip in, then publishes the lesson as your account. You stay anonymous unless you choose otherwise.
Describe vendors, products, and partners by category, not by name. This form does not collect the name of any other organization or person, and a reviewer removes any that slip in. The lesson is about your own decision and what you would do differently.
This lesson is published anonymously by default. You can choose to attribute it to your organization, to let interested readers reach out to you, or both. Each is a separate choice.

No GitHub account? Use Copy or Download and email it to danielle@boycedatascience.com.
