# Participate

If you'd like to help us test this resource, we'd genuinely value your time. Five testers per cycle is a small number, and each one shapes what changes before the next release.

## Who we'd love to hear from

We aim for a mix of perspectives in each testing cycle:

- **Clinicians** who diagnose or care for patients with genetic epilepsies — neurologists, clinical geneticists, genetic counselors, epileptologists.
- **Foundation and advocacy staff** working in any monogenic epilepsy community, whether your gene is on the list or not.
- **Patients and family members** with experience navigating a genetic epilepsy diagnosis.
- **Researchers** in academic or industry settings working on monogenic epilepsies or rare disease infrastructure more broadly.

You don't need to be an expert in this specific resource. You need to be willing to think aloud and be candid when something doesn't make sense.

## What to expect

- A 60–90 minute video call (or in person, if local to the Bay Area).
- An optional 10-minute feedback form afterward.
- No prep work. We'd actually prefer you not explore the site in advance — your first reactions are part of what we want to capture.
- Honest conversation, with permission to disagree, complain, or change your mind.

For more detail on what a session involves, see the [For testers](welcome-packet.md) page.

## What we offer

- A small thank-you in the form most appropriate to your situation — a gift card for patient and family testers, a modest honorarium for clinicians and researchers where institutional policy allows, or our straightforward gratitude where that's the right answer.
- A follow-up note after revisions are complete, showing what changed because of your feedback.
- The resource itself remains free and openly accessible — we don't sell access, we don't paywall content, and we don't run ads.

## What we won't do

- We won't use your name in any public output without your permission.
- We won't share session recordings outside our team.
- We won't keep you on a list for future asks unless you tell us you'd like that.

## Reach out

Email Danielle at [danielle@boycedatascience.com](mailto:danielle@boycedatascience.com) with a one-line note about who you are and which audience you most identify with. We'll reply within a few days with scheduling options for the next cycle.

If you've been forwarded this page by a foundation contact, please mention them when you reply — it helps us understand which channels are reaching people.
